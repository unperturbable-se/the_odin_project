Burger

Description

Burger is a very tasty and easy to make dish. in it's most basic form its just
2 patties between a piece of meat. ofcourse you can also add seasoning and lettuce etc.
inside as well.

Recipe

  • Cheese(optional)
  • Meat
  • Sauce
  • Bun

Steps

  1. Prepare and cook the meat
  2. Place everything on top of a bun
  3. Close it with a bun
  4. Cook it a little and your done!!!
