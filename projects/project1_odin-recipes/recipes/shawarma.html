Shawarma

Description

Shawarma is a middle eastern dish(originate from turkey) which is basically meat and sauces etc. wrapped with khubs bread

Recipe

  • Meat
  • Sauces
  • khubs bread
  • any thing els(optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the meat(bu cutting)
  2. add together everything and wrap it all up with khubs
  3. cook a little
  4. youre done!
